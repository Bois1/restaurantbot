Restaurant ChatBot

How to use:

1. Start by selecting an option from the menu below.
2. Type 1 to browse and add meals to your order.
3. Type 97 to view your current cart.
4. Type 99 to checkout and pay.
5. Type 98 to see your past orders.
6. Type 0 to cancel your current order.

All selections are made by typing the number and pressing Send.
